Submit a new price

You should make your price change submission at least 2 to 4 weeks before the effective date.

Submission process

Dictionary of medicines and devices (dm+d) is updated each Thursday. Updates to dm+d can be viewed on the following Monday. Only prices that have reached their effective date will be published in dm+d. Once accepted, it can take up to 6 days to appear on dm+d due to the weekly update schedule.

If a price submission is made less than 2 weeks before the effective date, it could take up to 3 weeks to appear on dm+d.

Checklist

In order to complete a submission you will need the following information.

  • Effective date
  • Product name
  • Pack size and UOM
  • New price
