Discontinue submission - What is the effective date of the discontinuation?

The date the product or pack is no longer available. This date cannot be in the past.

Information:

If submitting many products or packs, each must have the same effective date. Otherwise, a separate discontinue submission must be made.

Enter each element of the date as a number in the format dd mm yyyy.
