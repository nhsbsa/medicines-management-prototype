Have you got an authorised SmPC or Data Sheet?

An authorised SmPC is a summary of product characteristics for licensed medicines with Parts 8 and 9 completed. The product license number needs to begin with PL or PLGB.
A Data Sheet contains product name, ingredients and route (how it is taken) and is for unlicensed medicines.
Providing either option for the product makes sure information held is up-to-date.
