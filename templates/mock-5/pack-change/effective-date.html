Submit a product change - What is the effective date of the pack?

The date the pack is available in the warehouse and cannot be in the past. It can take up to 2 full weeks to show on dm+d. The extract day for dm+d is usually every Thursday.
Enter each element of the date as a number in the format dd mm yyyy.
