Set up two-factor authentication

To set up two-factor authentication, you need to download Authy or Google Authenticator. We recommend Authy as it will back up your code.

An authenticator is a secure method of keeping your account safe. The authenticator will generate a 6-digit code, and when you log in you will be prompted to enter your password as well as this code.

Where can I get Authy?

You can download Authy from the Google Play Store or the Apple App Store.

Where can I get Google Authenticator?

You can download Google Authenticator from the Google Play Store or the Apple App Store.

You do not currently have two-factor authentication enabled on your account. To enable it, please follow the steps below:

  1. Download and install your preferred authenticator app on your device.
  2. Scan the QR code or enter this code on the app you have downloaded.
  3. Enter the 6-digit code which the app generates below to enable.
