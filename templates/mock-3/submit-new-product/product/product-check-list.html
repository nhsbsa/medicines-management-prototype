Submit a new product

A new submission can take up to 2 weeks for the product to be accepted. You should make your product submission at least 2 to 4 weeks before the effective date (date the product is available in the warehouse).

Submission process

Dictionary of medicines and devices (dm+d) is updated each Thursday. Dm+d only contains products that have reached their effective date. Once accepted, it can take up to 6 days to appear on dm+d due to the weekly update schedule.

If a product is submitted less than 2 weeks before the effective date, it could take up to 3 weeks to appear on dm+d.

Checklist

In order to complete a submission you will need the following information.

  • Summary of Product Characteristics (SmPC)
  • pack information, such as pack size and type/form
  • Global Trade Item Number (GTIN)

As an optional task, to help with submitting a new product, please include:

  • images of packaging
  • pricing information
